✉️ Email 🎓 Google Scholar GitHub 📄 CV

I will be joining TikTok as a Senior Research Scientist. I am currently working with Prof. David Doermann at UB. I received my Ph.D. from LAMP, UCAS in 2024, advised by Prof. Qixiang Ye. B.E. from Jilin University (2019).

Research Focus

My research spans vision foundation models, multimodal LLMs, and efficient real‑time perception. I design architectures and training strategies that make models faster, simpler, and clearer for visual and multimodal signals.
